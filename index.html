01 - BIOGRAPHY and FOCUS

About Me

Dedicated researcher with a history of solving complex computer vision, speech processing, and trustworthy AI challenges.

Academic Overview

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu. Prior to this, I completed my MS/MPhil in Computer Science from the prestigious Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute of Engineering Sciences and Technology (GIKI), where I developed a strong background in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and intelligent computing.

My current research focuses on Deepfake Audio Detection, Deep Learning, Vision Transformers (ViTs), and AI for Low-Resource Languages, spanning speech processing, computer vision, and multimodal representation learning. My long-term research vision is to advance Large Language Models (LLMs), Vision-Language Models (VLMs), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and trustworthy foundation models that bridge cutting-edge research with real-world applications.

Alongside my research, I serve as a Lecturer in Computer Science at City University (CUSIT), where I teach programming, artificial intelligence, and machine learning while supervising undergraduate research and final-year projects. My broader goal is to translate academic research into practical, impactful AI solutions for society.
